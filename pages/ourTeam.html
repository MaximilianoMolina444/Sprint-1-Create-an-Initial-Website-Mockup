The Team

Max "Glacier" Henderson
Max is known for his calm under pressure and his ability to make spectacular saves in critical moments of the game. His nickname, "Glacier," comes from his composure even when the game gets intense. Outside of the ice, Max is a diligent student who enjoys science, dreaming of becoming an engineer someday.
Liam "Brick" McCoy
Liam is a sturdy and reliable defenseman who earns his nickname for being a true "wall" in defense. Taller than average for his age, he intimidates opposing attackers with his physical presence and ability to block shots. Liam loves spending time with his family and is passionate about action movies.
Derek "Grit" Walters
Derek is a tenacious defenseman known for his competitive spirit and knack for recovering the puck in tough situations. His nickname, "Grit," reflects his unyielding attitude and willingness to fight until the last second of the game. In his spare time, Derek enjoys fishing and is a member of his school's chess club.
Alex "Flash" Carter
Alex is an exceptionally fast and skillful center, capable of dodging defenders with ease. His nickname, "Flash," stems from his impressive speed and ability to score crucial goals. Alex is also a natural leader on the team and enjoys volunteering in his community.
Tyler "Hawk" Sanders
Tyler is an agile and strategic left winger, known for his vision and precision in passing. Dubbed "Hawk" for his ability to see and capitalize on opportunities on the ice, Tyler is also an avid reader who enjoys adventure and mystery books.
Dylan "Storm" Reed
Dylan is an explosive right winger, famous for his powerful shot and ability to create offensive plays. His nickname, "Storm," reflects his dynamic and sometimes unpredictable playing style. Off the ice, Dylan is a video game enthusiast who loves competing in local eSports tournaments.
